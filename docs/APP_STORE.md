## Description

100% local. 100% offline. 100% yours.

Every background is just slag waiting to be burned away. Foundry Slag is the AI-powered background removal tool that runs entirely on your Mac — no cloud, no subscription, no uploading your work to a stranger's server.

Drop an image into the forge. Foundry Slag fires up its AI furnace and strips the slag from around your subject in seconds. What remains is pure gold — a clean, edge-perfect cutout ready for Etsy listings, marketing materials, design layouts, or anywhere your work needs to shine.

**Smelt any image. Keep every pixel private.**
- Single images slag in 1–5 seconds on Apple Silicon
- Batch forge dozens of images in one pour — export the whole cast as a ZIP
- Fine-tune edges with Feather and Shift controls — soften, harden, or shift the cut line with precision
- Swap backgrounds with the color picker or cast a drop shadow beneath your subject
- Before/after comparison slider: drag across the crucible to inspect every edge
- Full history vault: every slagged image is archived and ready for re-export

**Six AI models. One offline forge.**
Choose your alloy: u2net for premium quality, u2netp for rapid-fire batches, isnet for studio-grade results, isnet-anime for illustration and character art, silueta for bold silhouette extraction, or u2net_human_seg for portrait and people work. Models temper themselves on first use — no manual setup required.

**Built for makers who move fast.**
Etsy sellers shooting product photos. Designers compositing campaigns. Marketers who need a dozen clean cutouts before noon. Foundry Slag is the forge for professionals who won't wait for a cloud's permission.

Export as PNG with transparency intact, WebP, or JPG with a custom background fill. Your originals never leave your machine. No telemetry. No phone-home. No surprises.

The slag burns away. The gold remains.
